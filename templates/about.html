About Me

Hello, I'm Chrissy Sitterson, LCSW-CP, and I'm delighted to share a bit about myself with you.
With a graduate degree from the University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA) and a strong
commitment to fostering holistic well-being, I've dedicated years to helping individuals on
their journey to healing and personal growth.

Beyond my professional identity, I am a wife and a mother of five wonderful children. This role
has provided me with a deep understanding of the joys, complexities, and challenges that come
with life's various chapters. It's a role that has also instilled in me a deep sense of empathy, as I
understand that each person's journey is unique.

With my years of experience and the personal insights I've gained, I strive to be not only a knowledgeable
therapist but also a compassionate and dedicated guide on your path to well-being. I'm here to support you,
no matter where you are in your journey, and to help you navigate life's challenges.

Thank you for considering me as your partner on this path to healing and growth.
